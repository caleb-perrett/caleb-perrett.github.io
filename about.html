A bit about me

I love sports, the outdoors and I love to travel and experience new cultures and peoples. Throughout my time I've lived in a few other countries, namely Singapore, Australia and Japan and have travelled to many more.

Why I'm studying at Dev Academy

In November 2019 I was offered an opportunity to sit an exam to get a job in the digital industry, while having no previous industry experience. I passed the exam and started work as a product owner for a startup tech company where I am currently working.

My boss originally suggested for me to take this course and I thought it was a good idea and good timing, as I've gained a lot in knowledge of the correct principles of building software but still lack a lot in the technicalities.

I'm hoping to increase my knowledge of web development languages and frameworks so I can be a more valuable asset within my company.
