My long term goal and career pathway

My long term goal as a software developer is to create or be a part of a team to create a piece of software that will be used by millions. When I finish this course I will become a junior developer in my company and will begin work as a developer on our current projects within my company, also while retaining my role as a product owner.

My strengths and limitations in relation to learning

One of my biggest strengths in relation to learning is my ability to set goals and stick at them until I achieve them. This is also the same with understanding hard concepts. I feel that this is one of the most important skills to have as a software developer as I believe the only thing that sets people who achieve great things apart from people who do not achieve great things is the time spent to achieve that thing.

A limitation I have can often be my ability to communicate my emotions effectively with others. I feel this is an important skill to master especially when working in a team environment.

Skills I would like to see developed while at EDA

The one and only reason why I'm studying at EDA is to improve my technical understanding of software development, and to do that as fast as possible. A huge part of this technical understand is developing a strong knowledge of Javascript.

How I will manage the workload while at EDA

I will manage my workload by getting it done.

Taking regular breaks from work is something I'm finding is key to understanding things. Having other hobbies is also very important to have a good work life balance. I enjoy getting into nature, boxing and working out. These are things that I prioritize.

How and where I will seek help in a timely way

I will seek help when needed.

What I expect from the facilitation team

I expect the facilitation team to provide help and resource when needed but to also allow for self directed learning.
